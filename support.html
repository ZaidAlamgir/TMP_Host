Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via PhonePe for our Indian readers (which includes UPI, Wallets, and Cards) and PayPal for our international readers. You will be able to choose your preferred method on the next screen.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your recurring subscription at any time through your account profile page. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. Please see our Cancellation & Refund Policy for more details.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. All payments are processed by trusted, secure payment gateways (PhonePe and PayPal). We do not collect or store any of your sensitive payment information on our servers. For more information, please read our Privacy Policy.
