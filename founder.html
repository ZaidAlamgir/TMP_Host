The Vision Behind TMP

Ammar Khan established the TMP News Agency with a singular, powerful goal: to create a media ecosystem that is fundamentally independent. In a landscape often dominated by corporate interests and government narratives, TMP stands apart.

As the parent company of The Muslim Post (tmpnews.com), the agency is dedicated to providing clarity on complex political situations within the Muslim world and across the globe.

Public Funded & Independent

TMP News Agency is a 100% public-funded initiative. We work solely for the public interest, empowered by our readers rather than advertisers or political stakeholders.

Expanding Our Reach

  • The Muslim Post: Our flagship English-language platform and verified App.
  • Upcoming Editions: Dedicated Hindi and Urdu versions are in active development to serve broader linguistic communities.These will operate on new domains but will remain core parts of the TMP News Agency family.
